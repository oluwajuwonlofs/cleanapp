About CleanApp

CleanApp is a web/mobile application that affords people with dirty laundry the opportunity to have their dirty laundry picked up, cleaned and delivered crisp from the comfort of thier homes or anywhere they find themselves in the Lagos Metropolis; Delivery could take 1-5 days depending on specifications (see terms and condition). The CleanApp is not limited to serving only users who wish to clean dirty laundry But also serves; Dispatchers and Drycleaners.

Dispatchers can therefore earn miniful pay on the CleanApp by virtue of signing up on the CleanApp. It is important to emphasis that dispatchers must take necessary integrity test in a bid to gather concrete informations of the dispatchers which is solely confidential to The cleanapp to ensure maxmum satisfaction and protection of 'Users' and their high-end laundry. CleanApp. 'Dispatchers' who pass the integrity test/check would be allowed or assigned to pick up dirty laundry and deliver back clean and crisp to 'Users' from the 'Drycleaners". (see....Select Categories)

Drycleaners i.e businesses like laundromarts and drycleaning services center(s) can harness the CleanApp for profit and to increase sales by leveraging 'Users" of CleanApp as its customers' for their laundromarts or drycleaning business. It is important to retraite clearly that Drycleaners by way of leveraging 'Users' of CleanAPP do not in anyway have direct contact with the 'User' whose laundry was dispatched by the 'Dispatcher'. ( see... Select Categories)

The CleanApp is infused with a pay-on-request payment system, such that eliminates not only situations where 'Users' don't pay for service; It also helps to avert unneccesarry arguments or confrontations between 'Dispatcher(s)' and 'User(s)' over pay. In addition, the payment system being infused in CleanApp, also evicts the bottleneck that Drycleaners face in that they no longer have to have a stock-pile of different clothings from different customers without pay for a long period of time,
